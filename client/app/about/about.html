About The Project

The FAA predicts that 7,500 unmanned drones will be operating in the U.S. by 2018. With Drone Pass we sought to create a prototype of a Drone Air Traffic Control system. Using GIS technologies, we created a system that allows landowners to set restrictions over their airspace and developed an autonomous FAA-like organization that monitors drone movements and reroutes their flight paths where needed.
